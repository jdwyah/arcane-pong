Arcane Pong Battle

⚔️ Player 1: Q (up), Z (down), A (attack spell), S (healing spell), D (upgrade laser)

🔮 Player 2: P (up), L (down), B (attack spell), N (healing spell), M (upgrade laser)

✨ Attack spells cost 1/4 mana and destroy segments of the opponent's magical barrier!

💯 Destroying all opponent segments gives you 50 bonus points!

💚 Healing spells require FULL mana and regenerate one of your own destroyed segments!

⬆️ Laser upgrade spells require FULL mana and permanently increase laser size by 20%!

🪄 Mana takes 20 seconds to fully recharge - choose wisely between attack and defense!
